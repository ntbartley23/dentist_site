About Know more about the event

LEXINGTON, Kentucky. March 6th. April is Oral Cancer Aware Month, and the SNDA community of University of Kentucky College of Dentistry partnered with the University of Louisville School Of Dentistry are leading a statewide awareness effort by encouraging the community to in our annual 5K run/walk. Each year these schools put aside rivalry to focus on community healthcare in Kentucky. Through this collaboration, these schools hope to see the disease get statewide attention and meaningfully raise public awareness. The more the public knows how to recognize the early signs of oral cancer, the better we can prevent unnecessary cancer deaths. The UK and UofL Oral Cancer Awareness 5K race will take place Saturday April 30th at (TIME) in Cold Stream Park in Lexington, Kentucky. It will be open to students and dental professionals from both institutions, community members and the public at large.
